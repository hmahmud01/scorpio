Scorpio Legacy's
Story

As Salamu Alaykum Wa Rahmatullahi Wa Barakatuh

السَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللهِ وَبَرَكَاتُه

May peace, mercy and blessings of Allah be upon you.

This is Tamjid and the story of Scorpio Legacy begins with me.

Headquartered at Dhaka in Bangladesh, the establishment year of Scorpio Legacy dates back to 2018 when it all began with my dream to establish a clothing brand to serve the people around me and give something back to the society by producing apparels that meet the staple needs of everyone from all spheres of life.

I believe it's time for a change and Scorpio Legacy is here to make a difference.

This feels wonderful to have two of my brothers, Mr. Leon and Mr. Ashraf on board who joined me on this beautiful journey to take Scorpio Legacy where we aspire to be and together we are surely stronger.

At Scorpio Legacy, we do things out of passion. We strive to find that specific break even point where standard quality meets reasonable price. We aim not only to improve the quality of your lifestyle, but also to give you the satisfaction of wearing comfortable, fashionable and elegant apparels.

Dreams do come true. Believe and you are halfway there.

It’s my mother and sister whom I always look up to and they have been instrumental for all accomplishments in my life. My father and brothers helped to make things easier for me and stood by my side when I needed them the most. Family will always be family and nothing in this world satisfies my heart more than being the middle son of my parents. Alhamdulillah. :’)

We are honored to begin our story leaving every uncertainty behind and we aim to go far and beyond with you. In shaa Allah.

Thank you for believing in us. We wish you success, prosperity and happiness in this life and in the hereafter.

Welcome to the family. Welcome to Scorpio Legacy.

Sincerely yours,

Salman Tamjid Quader

Founder & CEO
